"TYPES OF FLIPS"

FRONT FLIP

An act of rotating one's body 360 degrees in the forward direction is called front flip.

CARTWHEEL FLIP

a circular sideways handspring with the arms and legs extended is called cartwheel flip.

BACKHAND SPRING FLIP

A back handspring is a gymnastics maneuver that involves flipping backward, with hands touching the ground midway through the maneuver.
